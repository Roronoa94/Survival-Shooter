SURVIVAL SHOOTER

First Join files using File-Splitter 
Open file splitter and join Survival Shooter.7z.chunk001, 002 & 003

Install 7zip on your system and unzip the joined file.

1 -> Open Survival Shooter.
2 -> Keep your settings as you wish.
3 -> Start the game.
4 -> Use mouse to look around and up, down, right, left to move around.
5 -> Use left mouse button to shoot while aiming at zombies.
6 -> Kill as many zombies to gain score & avoid touching them.
7 -> Press alt+F4 to quit the game.

Happy hunting …………………..
